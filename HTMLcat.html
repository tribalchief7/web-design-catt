Background Information

KCA University (KCAU) is a private, non-profit institution. KCA University was founded in July 1989 by the institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (ICPAK).

Mission

To improve the quality of accountancy and financial manangement training in the country

Our Vision

To be the best private university in Africa.
To offer quality learning services
